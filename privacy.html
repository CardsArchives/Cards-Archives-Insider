Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 9, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how our card collection application (“the App”) handles user data. By using the App, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

The App does not automatically collect any personal information. All data you enter, including card details, price history, and optional images, is stored locally on your device.

2. Local Data Storage

Your collection information is saved using your device’s local storage (localStorage) or in-memory data while the App is running. We do not upload your data to our servers, and we have no remote access to it.

3. Images

Any images you choose to attach to a card — whether uploaded from your gallery, taken with your camera, or linked via URL — remain stored locally on your device or as part of your backup export file.

4. Price Information

Entering prices or price history is optional. This information is stored locally with your card data and is not transmitted to any external service.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or otherwise disclose your data to third parties.

6. Data Export and Import

The App allows you to export your collection data to a file and import it back at any time. These files remain under your control and are not sent to us. You are responsible for storing these files securely.

7. Children’s Privacy

The App is suitable for general audiences. If you are under 13 years old, you should use the App only with parental or guardian supervision. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Data Deletion

To delete all data stored by the App, you can use the in-app delete options (where available) or uninstall the App from your device. This will remove all stored card details, images, and price history from your device.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in the App. Any updates will be posted on this page with the revised effective date.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please contact us at: help.cardsarchives@gmail.com
